Overview

Purpose

Ducks are interesting creatures! They all love swimming and they all love the outdoors… but ducks all have different personalities. Have you ever met or seen a duck that is basically a comedian? Have you ever met a duck that is a quilting enthusiast? It’s okay, most people haven’t. Through this website, you will have met a duck that is really funny and loves to quilt. With this website, According to Chuck, you will know everything you need to know about ducks and quilting… and Chuck. Come along and learn all you can about Chuck the duck, what he loves, and what he hates! Have a quacktastic day!

Audience

My audience can be anyone who loves ducks! People of all ages can like ducks, you can be a grandparent, a grandchild, a college kid, etc. Ducks are for everyone! Quack!!
